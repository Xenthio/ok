Cooked Porkchop
Type: Item

Health Gain:

Description

Cooked Porkchop is created by cooking Raw Porkchop in a Furnace. Cooked Porkchop has a higher health gain than Raw Porkchop. You gain 4 Hearts of health by eating Cooked Porkchop.
