Description

Pickaxes can destroy Stone and Ore blocks faster than using fists or other tools. Certain type of Pickaxe are required to harvest the ores. Stone Pickaxes have 65 uses before they break and deal 1.5 Hearts of damage.

Stone Pickaxes can break Stone, Coal Ore and Iron Ore blocks.
