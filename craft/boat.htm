Type: Item
MC ID: 333

Description

Boats allow you to travel on water at speed. If they are crashed they break and drop Sticks and Wooden Planks. To enter a Boat Right Click on it. To exit a Boat Right Click outside the Boat. You can use the movement keys to move Boats. Boats can only be used on Water.
