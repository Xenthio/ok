Description

Because Fire can not be legaly obtained it is impossible to obtain Chainmail Armor without spawning either Fire or the Chainmail Armor. While wearing armor you take less damage. The differences between the types of Armor is how much durability it has. Chainmail Armor can take 96 hits. The more the Armor is damaged the less effective it is at protecting its wearer.
