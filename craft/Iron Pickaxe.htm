1
Type: Item
MC ID: 257

Description

Pickaxes can destroy Stone and Ore blocks faster than using fists or other tools. Certain type of Pickaxe are required to harvest the ores. Iron Pickaxes have 129 uses before they break and deals 2 Hearts of damage.

Iron Pickaxes can break all block types except Obsidian.
