Description

Shovels can destroy Dirt, Gravel and Sand blocks faster than using fists or other tools. The only difference between the shovel types is how meny uses you get out of them and how much damage they deal. Wooden Shovels have 33 uses before they break and deal 0.5 Hearts of damage.
